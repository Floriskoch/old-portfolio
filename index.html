Filling Pieces

Filling Pieces is a fast-growing Amsterdam-based footwear label, established in 2009.

My tasks were first and foremost building and maintaining their new webshop based on the OpenCart eCommerce platform. Apart from translating the design to a functional front-end I was also responsible for several REST/SOAP connections with third-parties, such as their warehouse.

Technologies used include:

  • - OpenCart, PHP-based eCommerce platform
  • - HTML5 / CSS3
  • - Gulp build system
www.fillingpieces.com

Marie-Stella-Maris

Marie-Stella-Maris is an Amsterdam-based lifestyle brand that believes everyone should have access to a source of clean drinking water.

I contributed setting up the front-end of the webshop based on OpenCart. Currently I'm co-responsible for maintaining the front and the back-end of the site.

Technologies used include:

  • - OpenCart, PHP-based eCommerce platform
  • - HTML5 / CSS3
www.marie-stella-maris.com

Field Reporter

Field Reporter is a visual reporting platform that allows organisations to capture and share their stories across the world.

Built the front-end of their portfolio website and integrated KirbyCMS to facilitate easy changes to the content. The site features an interesting parallax animation.

Technologies used include:

  • - Kirby, PHP-based CMS
  • - (Vanilla) JavaScript
  • - HTML5 / CSS3
fieldreporter.co
